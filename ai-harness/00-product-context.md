# Product Context

Product name: Payment Route Intelligence

One-line proposition: A future-facing banking demo where customers express payment outcomes and the bank recommends the safest executable route with clear evidence, controls and final approval.

## Customer Journey

1. Customer starts a secure payment session.
2. Customer expresses a payment outcome in natural language or mocked voice.
3. A trusted banking agent may help structure the intent in advice-only mode.
4. Customer confirms the structured payment intent.
5. A deterministic bank-owned route engine analyses safe routes.
6. The route engine recommends a route across traditional and digital rails.
7. GenAI explains the recommendation in customer-friendly language.
8. A representative payment journey map shows legs, controls, settlement boundaries and alternatives.
9. Customer final approval is required before execution.
10. The payment can then be tracked in the simulated demo.

## Trusted Agent Concept

The trusted banking agent can help structure intent and explain payment options. It cannot initiate, approve, execute, amend, cancel or move money. Bank-controlled context, tools, consent and audit boundaries are shown as mocked UI unless explicitly scoped otherwise.

## Deterministic Route Engine

The bank-owned route engine applies policy, controls, gates, scoring and deterministic route selection. It owns route recommendation and simulated execution state.

## GenAI Boundary

GenAI structures and explains. GenAI does not choose the route, score routes, approve execution, update payment state or move money.

## Representative Journey Map

The map is an expected/representative journey based on the selected route and decision trace. It is not live correspondent telemetry, real geocoding, real scheme telemetry or real FX data.

## Final Approval Boundary

No money moves until the customer approves. Final approval is customer-controlled. Passkey and agent concepts are mocked unless explicitly scoped later.
